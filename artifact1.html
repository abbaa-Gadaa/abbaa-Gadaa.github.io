AI Is Everywhere Now

Artificial intelligence is no longer a distant idea.

  • ChatGPT brought AI into everyday conversation
  • Coding copilots changed how developers work
  • Image generation turned prompts into visual creation
  • AI assistants are now part of how people learn, work, and create

What feels sudden today is the result of a journey that began more than seventy years ago.

Just over a decade ago, AI took a dramatic leap forward. What changed around 2012?

↓

When AI Learned on Its Own

2012 changed everything.

  • Before 2012, most AI systems followed fixed human-written rules
  • Deep learning allowed neural networks to learn from examples
  • Image recognition and speech understanding improved dramatically
  • More data and stronger hardware accelerated progress

Deep learning was the turning point — AI began learning patterns on its own.

But before 2012 and the deep learning revolution, how did AI systems work?

↓

Before AI Could Learn, It Followed Rules

Before deep learning, AI depended on human logic.

  • Engineers wrote explicit rules for how systems should behave
  • Logic trees and expert systems worked in narrow domains
  • These systems performed well in controlled environments
  • Real-world complexity made rule-based AI hard to scale

Early AI could be powerful, but only within the boundaries humans defined.

To understand how far back this idea goes, we have to go even further — to the moment people first began asking whether machines could think at all.

↓
1950

Where It All Began — Alan Turing, 1950

The origin of AI begins with a question.

  • In 1950, Alan Turing asked: Can machines think?
  • He published Computing Machinery and Intelligence
  • He introduced the idea behind what became the Turing Test
  • This question shaped the future of computing and AI

Long before modern models existed, Turing created the intellectual spark that started the field.

From one question in 1950, artificial intelligence evolved through decades of breakthroughs to become the systems we use today.
