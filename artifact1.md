# Artifact 1: Job Interview Assistant

## Title
Job Interview Assistant

## Introduction
This artifact presents an AI-based Job Interview Assistant created during the AI Lab. The project demonstrates how generative AI can be used to support interview preparation by helping users practice questions, organize responses, and improve confidence.

## Description
The Job Interview Assistant is a conversational AI tool designed to simulate interview preparation support. It helps users think through possible interview questions, structure responses, and engage in guided practice. This artifact represents an early example of applying AI to a practical, user-focused problem.

## Objective
The objective of this artifact was to explore how AI tools can assist with interview preparation. The project focuses on using conversational AI to guide users through interview questions and help them organize clear, structured responses.

## Process
The assistant was created during the AI Lab by designing prompts that guide a conversational AI system to behave like an interview preparation coach. The process involved defining the role of the assistant, structuring the types of questions it should ask, and testing responses to ensure the assistant provided helpful and realistic interview guidance.

## Tools and Technologies Used
- Generative AI chatbot
- Prompt engineering
- Web-based AI interface

## Value Proposition
This artifact demonstrates how AI tools can be applied to real-world problems such as interview preparation. It shows the ability to design AI-driven workflows that help users interact with technology in practical and meaningful ways.

## Unique Value
The assistant focuses on guiding users through interview preparation using conversational interaction. This approach highlights how AI systems can be used to simulate coaching and support users in developing better communication and preparation strategies.

## Relevance
Interview preparation is a common challenge for many professionals and students. AI-driven tools like this assistant can help individuals practice responses, gain confidence, and improve their readiness for real interview situations.
